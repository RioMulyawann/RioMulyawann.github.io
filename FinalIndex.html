About GrowFi

GrowFi (GFI) is a decentralized funding platform designed to solve a core problem in real economy financing: the difficulty for global MSMEs and startups, underserved by traditional financial institutions, to access capital. We utilize the advantages of Blockchain and Smart Contracts to create a secure, efficient, and completely transparent investment path.

Our Vision

To become the singular global platform recognized as the primary bridge between global capital and the multi-sector MSME real economy, achieving full financial inclusion supported by Artificial Intelligence (AI) risk scoring and a community-managed Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).

Our Mission

To change the global funding paradigm by building an inclusive and decentralized blockchain ecosystem. We aim to provide transparent, secure, and efficient capital access for millions of MSMEs/startups worldwide, while offering superior and protected investment opportunities for global investors through Crypto Collateral and Smart Contractguarantees.

Our Problem

Discriminatory Access to Capital

MSMEs/startups in developing countries are often rejected by banks and investors due to lack of credit history, physical collateral, and complex application processes.

Lack of Transparency and High Inefficiency

Traditional funding processes are opaque, making it difficult for investors to track capital use, while high borrowing costs are caused by middlemen and inefficiencies.

Minimal Crypto Investment Opportunities

Crypto investors struggle to find stable, real economy-linked investments in MSMEs, lacking transparency and clear risk mitigation.

Our Solution

GrowFi addresses these problems with a blockchain-powered platform that democratizes access to growth capital.

Crypto Collateral & Borderless Process

GrowFi eliminates bureaucracy and physical asset requirements. MSMEs provide collateral in the form of crypto assets (BTC, ETH, USDT) locked in Smart Contracts, ensuring security. The platform offers global access, connecting investors and MSMEs from all over the world.

On-Chain Ledger and Smart Contracts

All funding transactions, repayment statuses, and MSME fund allocations are recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency. Smart Contracts automate the process, reducing operational costs and improving capital efficiency.

Flexible Investment Models and Risk Mitigation

GrowFi offers diverse investment options, including lending models and profit-sharing models, with protection from crypto collateral and platform insurance to mitigate risks and offer stable returns.
